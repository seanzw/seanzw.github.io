About Me

I am a postdoc at UCLA, working with Prof. Tony Nowatzki. I am generally interested in developing high performance computer architecture for both general and domain specific computing with compiler support. I am also looking into how to accurately and efficiently model and simulate current architectures.

Prior joining UCLA, I received Bachelor of Engineering from Department of Electronic Enginerring, Tsinghua University. I also studied at ETH Zürich for half a year as a exhange student.
